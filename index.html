What does SPAMABLE mean ? ; It comes from SPAM + AMALGAMATE ( to spread and insert) .

In spanish “ESPAMABLE ” comes from “ESPACIAR” + ” AMAGAR”.

This is how I called this brand new design of furniture, basically chairs, armchairs and tables. It has the following characteristics:

1. That separates in two or more parts of equal or nearly equal (in milimiters of diference) size.

2. The separations are always produced by gliding

3. The set can be used as if it were one piece.

BIOGRAPHY

Lluís

Finishing his architecture degree, he trained and worked in one of the main structures calculation offices in Barcelona (Brufau,Obiol, Moya & Asociados).

From 1988 to 1994, conscious of the growing need for useul living space, invented several geometrical- mechanical systems that could possibly shape a new generation of furniture in a rational and constructive way ; the spamable furniture; chairs, armchairs and tables that can be splitted in another equal unit.

In year 2000 founded his own study of architecture and design, mostly focus un residential buildings.

In 2008 he won the international competition, in collaboration with the Algerian architect Nacer Belaaz, to design the LLeida Mosque capable of hosting 3.000 faithful in Ramadan.

In 2015 he began to specialize in photovoltaic installations, a discipline of which he is a professor and on which he is developing new and more economical solar tracking systems.

He has always considered himself an environmental activist, and regularly disseminates in conferences the problem of climate change and how we should act from home.
